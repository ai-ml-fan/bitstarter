Unico Toys is a Toy Hub for kids aged 4-12 years . We are closely watched and regulated - by kids. This is a kid-driven site. Every item we carry is child-safe *and* kid-approved and tested. Each of our toys are reviewed and rated by our panel of kids. We have a bunch of kids who are dedicated to the idea of making fun toys available to all kids.

Towards our motto of making toys available to all, we have a rental plan. Our customers can select toys and if they are not sure about their pick, they can check out their selection on a rental plan. If your child likes the toy he/she can keep it but if for some unusual reason, your child no longer likes the toy and wants to trade it, guess what, now you can. At Unico, we allow you to rent our toys which you can return and trade for a different toy.

FAQ

What is Unico Toys?
Unico is a Toy Hub where you can buy or rent toys and model sets. If you have a child who loves toys, Unico is for you. Or if you have a selective youngster and you have gone through the hassle of returning your picks more than once, Unico is for you. We offer a subscription plan that complements our online store. You can buy or rent from Unico. You can rent one,two or three sets or toys at a time. You can then choose to buy the set or toy. Or if your child is not satisfied, you can return it and choose a different set.
Can I sign up and try Unico Toys?
Yes, you can. We offer subscriptions starting as low as $8/month. Under this plan you can rent 1 Toy or set at a time. At the end of the month, you can sign-up for a monthly subscription plan. Under the monthly subscription, kids will be able to try out the latest, coolest toys in our collection. You can cancel your subscription anytime.
What toys or sets will I find on Unico Toys?
We carry an extensive collection of toys: Mattel, Caterpillar, Bruder, Fisher Price and other well-made toys. We also carry models such as LEGO, K'NEX, ERECTOR etc. The Unico collection of sets and toys are selected and hand-picked based on the popularity with kids. Our panel of kids love LEGO. So, our elaborate selection of LEGO sets include large sets(500+ pieces) as well as current popular themes - Friends, City, Ninjago, Chima etc. We encourage your participation. You can send us input on what brands and toys you would like to see on Unico.
How can I give feedback or suggestions?
We welcome your suggestions and feedback. Please email us at info@unicotoys.com.
