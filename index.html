Unico Toys is a Toy Hub for kids aged 4-12 years.

Unico Toys was started to solve a need of kids. Kids have a hard time determining which toys they like. They want to play and try-out different toys and sets before deciding which of those they would like to build on.

For instance, take the case of LEGO.

LEGO has categories: Duplo, Friends, Ninjago, Star Wars etc. Our LEGO-crazy kids and their friends want to play with sets from different themes and then decide if they want to keep it.

Towards our motto of making toys accessible to all, we have a rental plan that complements our online store. You can purchase toys in our store. But if you are not sure about your selection, you can check out your selection on a rental plan.

In addition, you can choose to buy the toy or set. If your little one decides they cannot part with it,he/she can keep it and you can convert your transaction to a purchase. But if for some unusual reason, your child does not like the toy and wants to try some other toy, guess what, now they can.At Unico, you can choose to rent one,two or three toys at a time on a subscription plan.

This is a kid-driven site. We have a bunch of kids who are dedicated to the idea of making toys easily available to all kids. We are closely watched and regulated- by kids. Each of our toys are reviewed and rated by our panel of kids. Every item we carry is child-safe *and* kid-approved and tested.

FAQ

What is Unico Toys?
Unico is a Toy Hub where you can buy or rent toys and model sets. If you have a child who loves toys, Unico is for you. Or if you have a selective youngster and you have gone through the hassle of returning your picks more than once, Unico is for you. We offer a subscription plan that complements our online store. You can buy or rent from Unico. You can rent one,two or three sets or toys at a time. You can convert from a rental to a purchase if you decide to keep the set or toy. Or continue on a monthly rental plan.
Can I sign up and try Unico Toys ?
Yes, you can. We offer subscriptions starting as low as $8/month. Under this plan you can rent 1 Toy or set at a time. At the end of the month, you can sign-up for a monthly subscription plan. Under the monthly subscription, kids will be able to try out the latest, coolest toys in our collection. You can cancel your subscription anytime.
What toys or sets will I find on Unico Toys ?
We carry an extensive collection of toys: Mattel, Caterpillar, Bruder, Fisher Price and other well-made toys. We also carry models such as LEGO, K'NEX, ERECTOR etc. The Unico collection of sets and toys are selected and hand-picked based on the popularity with kids. Our panel of kids love LEGO. So, our elaborate selection of LEGO sets include large sets(500+ pieces) as well as current popular themes - Friends, City, Ninjago, Chima etc. We encourage your participation. You can send us input on what brands and toys you would like to see on Unico.
Can my kid be part of the panel ?
Our toys and sets are tested and choosen by our panel of kids. You can drop us a line and let us know of your interest. We would like to have active participation from kids and welcome their suggestions and reviews. We have a Chief Child Officer(CCO) and Chief Child Play Officer(C2PO) who represent the panel and are very active in the strategy and plans for Unico Toys.
How can I give feedback or suggestions ?
We welcome your suggestions and feedback. Please email us at info@unicotoys.com.
My child cannot wait to subscribe to your sets. When are you launching your store ?
We expect to launch our online store by Fall of 2013. Please drop us a line at info@unicotoys.com and we will add you to our mailing list. Also please do let us know the sets or toys that you would like to see in our store.
